About


Here at Helping Hands, we aim to enable and promote community involvement by providing easy access to a variety of volunteer opportunities and information to our users! By matching your personal interests with relevant volunteer opportunities in your community, gain valuable experience and help your neighbors!

Inspiration


Whether you are new to community involvement and are not sure of where to start or are new to a community, Helping Hands hopes to bridge the gap between willing community members and volunteer event coordinators by providing a one-stop site for users.

What We Offer


COMMUNITY

Get to know your neighbors and have a positive impact! Whether in your own community or another nearby, help make a difference!

GREEN-FOCUS

Explore green opportunities within your own community! Find ways to increase reusability and promote sustainability!

POSITIVE EXPERIENCE

Gain experience in your desired field or area of expertise! From tutoring to community clean-ups, find your fit!


CERTIFIED USERS

A safe environment for volunteers and organizers! You have the choice to get involved or wait until you find your niche!

INSTANT UPDATES ON OPPORTUNITIES

Access new opportunities from wherever you are and find your favorite activity!

HARD WORK

Be proud of doing the hard work and making a difference! See your community from a new point of view!
